Understanding
Coffee Circuit

Coffee producers are often lost along specialty coffee supply chains. We allow producers to be seen and heard in the stories they want to tell and heard the way they want to be, without curation, censorship, or filters. We allow roasters to connect with producers they might never meet otherwise, and to know them in ways that are real and authentic.

A circuit is “a two-way communication path between points”

Transparency happens when there are no obstructions.

What It Is

  • A place for producers to communicate directly with roasters
  • A place for roasters to learn directly about producers

What It Does

  • Allows producers to share their own farm stories
  • Allows roasters to hear from, and communicate with, producers

What It Isn't

  • A profit making entity
  • A place to sell or buy coffee
  • A trading company

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Coffee Circuit?

Coffee Circuit serves as a means for farmers who only have access to local intermediaries to connect with roasters, and alternatively, a way for roasters to meet farmers authentically, with no curation, staging, or marketing.

How does Coffee Circuit work?

Farmers can create profiles to share their work, stories, and harvests, owning their identities. Roasters also share pertinent information and can express interest in working with farmers. Farmers decide whether it’s a good fit. They can talk with one another in a free but facilitated environment.

What does Coffee Circuit actually do?

It allows farmers whose identities are normally lost in the supply chain the chance to be seen and heard the way they want to be, without curation, censorship, or marketing filter. It allows roasters to connect directly with farmers who normally would never find, even if they travel to farming communities. Coffee Circuit provides farmers with a space to make themselves visible and known Facilitates direct communication and collaboration between smallholder farmers and roasters
