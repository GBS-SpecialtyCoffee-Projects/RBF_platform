Understanding Coffee Space

It allows farmers whose identities are normally lost in the supply chain the chance to be seen and heard the way they want to be, without curation, censorship, or marketing filter. It allows roasters to connect directly with farmers who normally would never find, even if they travel to farming communities.

Connecting, not trading

You don't need transparency when there are no barriers
What It Is

A means for farmers who only have access to local intermediaries to connect with roasters A way for roasters to meet farmers authentically, with no curation or staging

What It does

Provides farmers with a space to make themselves visible and known Facilitates direct communication and collaboration between smallholder farmers and roasters.

What It Isn't

The attempt to make an amazon of coffee A trading company A “direct trading” platform A profit-making business
